Shopify Alternatives for European Entrepreneurs in 2026

Shopify dominates the global e-commerce market. With over 4 million webshops worldwide, it’s the go-to solution for many starting entrepreneurs. But is Shopify also the best choice for European webshops? More and more entrepreneurs are discovering better alternatives.

Why European Entrepreneurs Are Leaving Shopify

Transaction Fees

Shopify charges transaction fees on top of your payment provider’s costs — unless you use Shopify Payments. In Europe, Shopify Payments isn’t available everywhere, meaning you pay double.

GDPR Compliance

Shopify’s servers are in the US. For European webshops, this brings GDPR challenges. Data needs to be on European servers, and Shopify doesn’t always make this easy.

Multilingual Support

Shopify’s multilingual support is limited. You need expensive apps to offer your webshop in multiple languages. And even then, SEO per language is often suboptimal.

The Alternatives

1. WooCommerce (WordPress)

Pros: Open source, huge plugin library, full control
Cons: Technical knowledge needed, self-host, security risks
Best for: Tech-savvy entrepreneurs who want full control

2. Shopware

Pros: German platform, GDPR-compliant, strong B2B features
Cons: Steep learning curve, more expensive than Shopify
Best for: Medium to large European webshops

3. PrestaShop

Pros: Open source, French platform, European community
Cons: Dated interface, fewer plugins than WooCommerce
Best for: Budget-conscious entrepreneurs in Southern Europe

4. ShopVirge

Pros: Automatic multilingual, European hosting, no transaction fees, live in 1 hour
Cons: Newer platform, smaller ecosystem
Best for: SMB entrepreneurs who want to go international fast
